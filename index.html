What we stand for

At Mdm Ling Bakery, gifting is the whole point. We design gift sets that are sustainable, heartfelt and genuinely useful, the kind kept and reused long after the mooncakes are gone. Every piece carries a little of Singapore's heritage with a modern spirit, made for the simple, universal act of bringing something to share.

Signature Assorted Baked Mooncakes

Modern skins, natural colour
Our signature assorted range carries the modern side of Mdm Ling Bakery. The Momoyama skin takes its colour from natural ingredients rather than the baked brown of a traditional skin, giving each flavour its own hue and a softer bite. It's where we let the flavours play.
Flavour 1

Belgian Double Chocolate

✓ Halal🌱 Vegetarian

A Momoyama skin and filling both made with Belgian chocolate, for a deep cocoa richness from the first bite to the last.

Flavour 2

Black Sesame Peanut Butter

✓ Halal🌱 Vegetarian

Toasty black sesame meets smooth peanut butter, a nostalgic pairing carried in a soft Momoyama skin.

Flavour 3

Emerald Pandan Golden Yolk

✓ Halal

Fragrant pandan in an emerald Momoyama skin, with a golden salted yolk at its heart. A local favourite, reimagined.

Flavour 4

Red Dates Longan

✓ Halal🌱 Vegetarian

Warm red dates and longan, two ingredients long loved for comfort and good wishes, folded into a rose-hued Momoyama skin.

Storage & freshness

Keep both ranges in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. We highly recommend enjoying them as soon as possible for maximum freshness and satisfaction, as they're made without any preservatives.
