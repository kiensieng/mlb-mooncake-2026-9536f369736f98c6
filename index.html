Storage & freshness

Keep both baked ranges in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. We highly recommend enjoying them as soon as possible for maximum freshness and satisfaction, as they're made without any preservatives.

Snowskin mooncakes are best served chilled. Keep them away from direct heat and sunlight, and never leave them in the car boot. Within 2 hours of purchase, place them in the freezer at −12°C or below, where they'll keep for up to 8 weeks. Once thawed, do not refreeze.

Leather Gift Bag

The Dawn 花晨

花晨 huā chén · flower morning

Soft as first light, The Dawn is a cream leather bag made to be carried long after the celebration ends.

Its gentle, neutral tone and supple finish move easily from a festive visit to everyday wear, a quiet kind of luxury that never asks for attention.

Its name comes from the old idiom 花晨月夕, flower mornings and moonlit evenings, the two loveliest hours of any day. We split it across a pair of bags: The Dawn is 花晨, the morning the flowers open, in cream like first light.

Designed as a keepsake rather than a wrapper, The Dawn turns a Mid-Autumn gift into something kept, used and remembered for years to come. Pair it with The Dusk, its warmer companion, for two halves of the same day.

Leather Gift Bag

The Dusk 月夕

月夕 yuè xī · moonlit evening

Warm as the last hour of daylight, The Dusk is a terracotta leather bag with a richer, deeper character than its cream counterpart.

Its earthy, sunset tone carries a sense of occasion while staying wearable well beyond the season, as at home with a weekend coat as with a celebration.

The Dusk is 月夕, the moonlit evening from the idiom 花晨月夕, the other of the day's two loveliest hours. Where The Dawn wears first light, The Dusk wears the last of it, and together the pair bookend the day the way family bookends a life.

More heirloom than packaging, The Dusk lets your Mid-Autumn gift live on as a bag that's reached for again and again. The natural other half to The Dawn.
